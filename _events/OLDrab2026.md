### Example Event
As part of this initiative, we will organize a series of academic sessions with invited researchers to discuss topics such as developing competitive research proposals, strategies for scientific publishing, building research networks, and fostering innovation in research. The sessions will feature international speakers with experience in research leadership and project evaluation, creating opportunities for meaningful exchange and collaboration.

The event will be conducted in a bilingual format (Spanish–English) to welcome participants from different countries and encourage broader academic dialogue. This effort is also connected to the mission of QMéxico, which promotes science, research, and collaboration across the Americas by strengthening connections among researchers and supporting the development of new scientific initiatives in the region.

- Role: Speaker
- Topic: Research and colloboration
- Institution: International insitutions representation.
